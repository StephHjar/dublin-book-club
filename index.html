About Us

The Dublin Book Club was founded in 2020, as a way to connect with fellow book lovers. Each meeting is hosted by a member of the club, who will come prepared with questions to ask, which prompt discussion about the book we've read - and there is also plenty of time to socialize as well!

Book selections are generally adult fiction novels, with some genre fiction sprinkled in. At the end of each meeting, the host member will suggest 3 book options, and the rest of the group will vote on the next book. Curious?
